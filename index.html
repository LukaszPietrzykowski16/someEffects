Saul goodman

James Morgan "Jimmy" McGill, better known by his business name Saul Goodman and also later by the assumed identity of Gene Takavic, is the titular protagonist of the series Better Call Saul, a prequel/sequel spinoff of Breaking Bad, in which he is also a main character. He is primarily portrayed by Bob Odenkirk, and was created by Vince Gilligan and Peter Gould.
